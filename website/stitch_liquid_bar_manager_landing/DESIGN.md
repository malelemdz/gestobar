# Liquid Modernist Design System (Dark Mode)

## Brand Identity
A premium, dark, and sophisticated aesthetic designed for bar and nightlife management. The style uses "Extreme Rounded" corners (24px-32px), subtle borders, and a vibrant cyan-indigo-pink accent palette against a deep charcoal background.

## Color Palette
- **Background (liquidBg):** `#14161A`
- **Surface (liquidSurface):** `#24262A` (Cards, level 1 surfaces)
- **Surface Low:** `#1E2024`
- **Surface High:** `#2E3035`
- **Primary:** `#00BCCF` (Premium Cyan)
- **Secondary:** `#6366F1` (Premium Indigo)
- **Tertiary:** `#EC4899` (Premium Pink)
- **On Surface:** `#EBEBEF` (White-Grey)
- **On Surface Variant:** `#CBE2E3` (Teal-Grey)
- **Outline:** `#4A5657` (Subtle 1px borders)
- **Success:** `#10B981`
- **Danger:** `#EF4444`
- **Warning:** `#F59E0B`

## Typography
- **Font Family:** 'Poppins', sans-serif
- **Display Large:** 48px, Weight 800 (Metrics, Hero titles)
- **Headline Large:** 32px, Weight 700 (Section titles)
- **Headline Medium:** 24px, Weight 700 (Component titles)
- **Headline Small:** 20px, Weight 700 (Card titles)
- **Body Large:** 16px, Weight 400 (Paragraphs)
- **Body Medium:** 14px, Weight 400 (Standard text)
- **Label Large:** 12px, Weight 600 (Caps/Metadata)
- **Label Small:** 10px, Weight 600 (Secondary metadata)

## Component Rules
- **Cards:** Background `#24262A`, Border 1px `#4A5657` (40% opacity), Radius 24px.
- **Buttons:** Primary cyan background, indigo/pink accents. Rounded-full or 24px radius.
- **Inputs:** Dark backgrounds, outline focus in Primary cyan.
- **Navigation:** Transparent or dark background, high-contrast links.
- **Modals:** Centered, deep overlay, matching card styles.

## Layout Patterns
- **Grid:** Modern bento-grid layouts for metrics and features.
- **Spacing:** Generous whitespace to maintain a "liquid" premium feel.
- **Responsive:** Fluid containers, mobile-first stacking.
